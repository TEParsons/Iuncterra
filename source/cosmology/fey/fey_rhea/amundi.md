# Amundi

![Amundi](amundi.png)

Tiny pieces of the wrath of [Amun](/cosmology/fey/fey_nova/amun), scattered throughout the desert of [Nasruk](/places/nasruk), the Parbati mountains and the Satru mountains. 


```statblock:5e
### Amundi
[Fey rhea](/cosmology/fey/fey_rhea#bonding)

#### Idle effect
If known, upgrades the cantrip [Sacred Flame](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618967-sacred-flame). If not known, grants the cantrip.

#### Activation effect
Cast [Continual Flame](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619065-continual-flame) for free.

#### Summoning effect
Cast [Wall of Fire](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619107-wall-of-fire) for free.

Summoning with more amundi increases the damage by 1d8 and the length of the wall by 15ft. (5ft. diameter).
```
