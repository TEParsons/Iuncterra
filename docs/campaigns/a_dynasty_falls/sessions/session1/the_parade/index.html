The Parade

Players introduce their characters and meet Unna Kausala. She briefs them on their first mission together: To disrupt a military parade, lead by Pasha Yapmak, in Jami-Satru. How they do this is up to them, but it needs to be public: The point of the parade is to showcase the military power of the Sultan. It needs to be seen to be opposed.
