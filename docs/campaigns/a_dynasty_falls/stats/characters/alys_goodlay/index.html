Charm. One humanoid within communication range must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or be charmed for 1 day.

Draining Kiss. Kiss a charmed or willing creature. The target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 3d10 + 3 psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The target's hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the damage taken. This reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest. The target dies if this effect reduces its hit point maximum to 0.
