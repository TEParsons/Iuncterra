Born from the union of Nomon and Hyron, the many headed hydra is a recurrent menace to the people Kaiper. It has the remarkable ability to regrow its heads when one is cut off; so to kill it fully one would have to cut off every head at the same time, without allowing others to grow back. This is a dangerous business, as each one cut off with grow back as two heads.
