Atkani

ætkɑni

The atkani are the people of Ordo'Atkan, nomadic horse archers who dominate the Atkani Step. While most atkani are orc, the native people of the region, the label of atkani is open to all who practice the "Way Of The Open Sky", the quasi-religious belief of the atkani people.

The Way Of The Open Sky

The atkani worship the Skyfather, known to them as Tenger, as the fey from whom the sky is manifest. Living in wide open plains with a dry climate, the vastness of the sky above is of great cultural importance to the atkani.
