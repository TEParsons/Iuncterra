Ordo'Atkan

ɔdəʊ ætkɑn

East of Kashar lies a land of wide open planes where the nomadic Atkani people roam. The Atkani are primarily and historically Orcish, but the horde considers anyone who lives by the "Way Of The Open Sky" to be equally of their people, regardless of species.
