Kash Tarcata

The last good Sultan, Tarcata commanded respect and garnered loyalty. A capable and fair ruler, under him Kashar went from strength to strength. He invested heavily in public works and infrastructure, literally paving the roads of the future. His son, Kash Pasanhara, does not share his talent for leadership.
