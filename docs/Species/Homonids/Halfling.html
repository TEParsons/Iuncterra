Far to the west of Iuncterra Proper lies a large island of rolling, emerald hills and pleasant floral meadows: Átaitha. Here live the small and cheerful folk, chosen people of Ludonysos, who enjoy the plentiful bounty of the island and take great pleasure in food and song and good company, far away from the great happenings of the wider world.
