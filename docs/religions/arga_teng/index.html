Arga Teng

The "way of the open sky" encompasses both a theology which venerates Ouron as supreme among all other celestials, and a nomadic pastoral lifestyle centred around animal husbandry (horses in particular), archery, combat and stoicism.

Arga teng is intimately linked with life in the Atkani steppe, blending sacred practices with the everyday routines necessary for survival in the vast grasslands.
