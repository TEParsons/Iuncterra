Aonai

Seraph of pride

Aonai embodies the good aspects of pride; a quiet confidence and self-assuredness, a strong sense of self identity and positive belief in oneself.

The Two Faced God

Aonai is irrevocably joined to his inverse, Ianoa, who embodies the negative aspects of pride. When in the material realm, the two appear as conjoined, each controlling one arm, one leg, and one tail.
