Fey Tíra

Fey Tíra, sometimes called Fey Lords, are manifest as a particular region or a large geographical feature. Jibana, for example, is manifest in the mighty Jibana river. Jor is manifest as the north sea. Veldor is the Veldspine mountain range. Fey Tíra wield significant power; capable of causing landslides, floods and storms, but also ferrying travellers safely, concealing armies and creating swathes of fertile farmland.
