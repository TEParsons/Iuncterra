Amundi

Tiny pieces of the wrath of Amun, scattered throughout the desert of Nasruk, the Parbati mountains and the Satru mountains.

Statblock (5e)

Amundi

Fey rhea

Idle effect

If known, upgrades the cantrip Sacred Flame. If not known, grants the cantrip.

Activation effect

Cast Continual Flame for free.

Summoning effect

Cast Wall of Fire for free.

Summoning with more amundi increases the damage by 1d8 and the length of the wall by 15ft. (5ft. diameter).
